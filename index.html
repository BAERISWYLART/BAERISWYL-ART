About This Work

Silence No. 3 belongs to a series exploring the weight of unexpressed language — the things held, swallowed, kept quiet. The surface works in layers: deep brown-black grounds built up with palette knife, a horizontal scar of gold leaf crossing the lower third of the composition.

The gold does not decorate. It marks. A line of something precious surviving through pressure — not triumph, but endurance. The crack lines running vertically through the upper register are intentional: pressure expressed as structure.

This work is for a room that should hold atmosphere, not simply fill space.

Interior Placement

Works best in environments with neutral walls — ivory, warm grey, dark charcoal. The gold carries across the room. Suited to living rooms, private studies, and collectors' environments with considered lighting.

Shipping is arranged individually after acquisition confirmation. Works are professionally packed and fully insured. A secure payment link is provided once availability and delivery terms are confirmed. Certificate of authenticity and acquisition documentation included.

Private Advisory

A curated selection service for collectors, interiors, and rooms that should not look accidental.

The right painting does not announce itself. It settles. It changes the proportion of a room, the quality of the silence, the way the space feels at different hours of the day. BAERISWYL ART offers a private selection service for those who want to choose deliberately — not by trend, not by price alone, but by instinct informed by knowledge.

For new collectors

Your first serious work is the most important decision. We help you understand what you are looking for before you look — developing your eye, your criteria, and your confidence.

For interiors

We work with the specific dimensions, light quality, palette, and atmosphere of your space to identify works that will anchor rather than decorate it.

For statement pieces

Some rooms need one defining work — a painting that does not compete but settles all questions of tone and hierarchy. We find it.

For gifts

An original painting is one of the few gifts that says something about both the giver and the receiver. We help you choose with precision and elegance.

For existing collectors

Building a coherent collection over time requires discipline, distance, and occasional external perspective. We offer private collection review and acquisition guidance.

For corporate interiors

Offices and hospitality environments that require works of presence, quality, and coherent visual language — curated with the same discipline as a private collection.

The Platform

"Art is not an accessory. It is a decision — and the best decisions come from knowing what you value, what you can see, and what you are willing to live with."

BAERISWYL ART was built from a simple conviction: that the experience of acquiring original art should feel as considered, as intelligent, and as private as the act of collecting itself.

The platform exists for people who are serious about the paintings they choose to live with — whether they are building a first collection or a fifteenth. It is not designed for volume. It is designed for clarity.

Every work in the collection is selected with editorial precision. The criteria are not market-driven. They are based on emotional depth, visual authority, conceptual strength, and the specific quality of presence a painting brings to a room.

The name BAERISWYL ART carries a simple idea: that taste is not decoration. It is judgment. And judgment — in art, in life, in the rooms we choose to inhabit — is one of the most underrated forms of intelligence.

We believe art changes the psychology of the person living with it. Not because it is beautiful — though it may be — but because it holds something. A decision. A memory. A position.

Based in Switzerland. Thinking internationally. Selecting deliberately.
